Why Marquees are the Event-Space Equivalent of a Blank Canvas 🎨

First things first—have you ever stepped into a venue that should work for your event, but there’s just something that doesn’t quite sit right? Maybe it’s the layout—it’s too cramped or too sprawling. Maybe it’s the décor—someone else's leftover Christmas decorations… or someone's very daring art attempts (😬). But with a marquee, you don’t have to settle for someone else's vision. This space? It’s yours to create, baby.


1. Catered to Your Needs (No Compromises!)

London has a unique vibe, and so do the people who live here. If you’re someone who likes to do things your own way (and let’s face it, who doesn’t!?), a marquee offers unlimited potential to shape a bespoke atmosphere that reflects your vibe.


  • Event size: Hosting a close-knit family affair? Or maybe your guest list is long enough to rival a Glastonbury lineup? Don’t sweat it, because marquees let you scale up or down. Want more space? Extend the marquee. Tight on room? Yup, shrink it. All the flexibility, none of the hassle.
  • No design rules: Forget the maddening struggle of choosing venues with conflicting aesthetics—you can make your marquee ANYTHING you want. Bold colors, sleek modern designs, hanging florals, posh chandeliers (add a disco ball if that’s your jam). The marquee gives you full throttle creative control to craft an environment that suits the occasion perfectly.

2. Weather? Who Cares! London’s Got Nothing on Your Event 🌧️☀️

London weather. Ugh. Need I say more? One minute, the sun is beautifully shining, the next, the heavens open up like it’s monsoon season. Welcome to ye ol' British wild card.

But here’s where marquees really shine: the weather is no longer your enemy. Beaumont Marquees prides itself on providing structures that can handle any unexpected atmospheric drama, ensuring your balloons won’t fly away, puddles won’t form puddles, and guests won’t have to flee for shelter. 💨🛶

And get this—if it swelters? Think cool breezes wafting through open sides. Cold, grey, and gloomy? Your cozy tents can be heated, twinkling with lovely warmth to keep everyone snug while they chat, dance, or host impromptu philosophical debates over cocktails (hey, it happens).

Point is: marquees are ready for anything your event day throws at it. And in London, that’s a huge deal.

Introducing Beaumont Marquees - London’s Premier Event Architects 💪

Beaumont Marquees isn’t a one-size-fits-you-if-we-say-so kind of operation. They let you pick from a wide range of marquee styles. So, whatever your vibe, they have a marquee hire that matches—not one that kinda-sorta fits, but a marquee that checks every box on your vision board.


  • Traditional pole marquees: Got a soft spot for vintage style, countryside chic, or princess-dream weddings? You’ll love these. (Think regal tea parties and Downton Abbey-style sophistication.)
  • Clearspan marquees: Open spaces, tons of natural light, stunning panoramic views... All without any support poles awkwardly breaking up the scene! If you want something sleek and modern to blend with urban landscapes (🏙️), this one’s for you.
  • Stretch tents: Boho? Edgy? Gorgeous yet informal? The stretch tent’s oozing with cool that none can handle. It reminds me of festivals, where you’re surrounded by creativity and a refreshingly chill vibe.

Basically, Beaumont Marquees has the marquee you need, no matter what.

You’re already stressing about the DJ, the caterers, and whether Great Uncle Geoff is going to cause another scene with his “dad joke phase.” The last thing you need is more hands-on logistics to drive you up the wall.

When you partner with Beaumont:


  • You say what you want, and they set it up to perfection.

Honestly, this is a personal lifesaver. You’re giving yourself the freedom to enjoy the event, not just survive it.

We all know how important it is to make an impression. Whether it's for work or play, a marquee from Beaumont Marquees doesn’t just give your event a space—it becomes part of the experience itself.

Picture this: Guests walk across a perfectly manicured lawn, and there it is—a glittering marquee that looks straight out of a Vogue spread. It’s gorgeously styled to a tee, with elegant lighting casting a golden glow and every little detail thought of.
